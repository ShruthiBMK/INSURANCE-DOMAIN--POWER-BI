# INSURANCE-DOMAIN--POWER-BI
## Shield Insurance Analytics ‚Äì Problem Statement

Shield Insurance aims to better understand its customer base, revenue generation, and policy performance by leveraging data insights. Due to rapid market growth and changing customer demographics, the company requires a detailed analysis to monitor business performance, customer trends, and policy preferences.

This pilot project focuses on building a structured data analytics solution to evaluate key business metrics such as total customers, total revenue, customer growth rate, and revenue growth rate. Additionally, segmentation by age group, city, and sales mode will help the company identify business opportunities and optimize marketing strategies.

By developing interactive dashboards and data models, the goal is to uncover actionable insights, improve decision-making, and strengthen confidence in data-driven business strategies.
